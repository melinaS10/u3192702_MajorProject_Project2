About virtual Gallery

The virtual gallery is a tribute to three of my favourite artists: Pablo Picasso, Salvador Dali and Vincent Van Gogh.It encourages users to interact with each artwork by using simple CSS animations, interactive commands and reveals information as they navigate around the works.
